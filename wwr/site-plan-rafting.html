Overview

Purpose

Rafting has a way to enrich our lives and provide us with great adventures. Our goal is to take those experiences and share them with our community and fellow rafting lovers. Regardless of the level of proficiency, all are welcomed in this family. Share your experiences and tips with one another and let us learn together and appreciate the beauty found in rafting.

Audience

We are focused on helping our rafters create a sense of community and provide those experiences for this group. Also, we are opened for those who want to try rafting for the first time and with their families.
